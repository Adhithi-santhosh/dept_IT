Message From The HOD

The Department of Information Technology was started in the year 2000 with an undergraduate B.Tech course, and a postgraduate programme, M.Tech with a specialization in Network Engineering, started in the year 2013. The department functions with eminent and dedicated faculty members and supporting staff and is equipped with established lab facilities and smart classrooms. Our Department focuses on outcome-based education which emphasizes placement by providing quality education. Various training programmes, remedial classes, poster presentations, and GATE coaching classes are organized throughout the year. Our faculty members and students are deeply engaged in research work, and five of our student projects got approval for funding in this academic year.
